Summer Internships for students of B.Sc.(Hons.) Mathematics


Summer Internships are offered by all the institutes having M.Sc.(Hons.) Mathematics as one of their courses.
IITs, IISERs, NISERs, ISI, TIFR being some of them.
Registerations for the same begin February onwards.

Detailed Information and more institutes regarding the same will be available February onwards.
